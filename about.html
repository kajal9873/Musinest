About Musinest

Musinest is a mini music player web project developed as a part of frontend development practice. It blends modern design and functionality to offer a smooth and engaging music listening experience.

Features

  • 🎵 A clean, attractive layout displaying a curated list of songs.
  • 🎧 Full control to play, pause, forward, and backward music easily.
  • 📊 Draggable progress bar to jump to any part of the song.
  • ❤️ Like your favorite songs with a simple Like button.
  • 🎶 Responsive and visually styled UI with smooth interactions and background visuals.
